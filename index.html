django-grappelli

The Django admin interface is a powerful application in order to manage your websites data — some might call it a CMS. On top of Djangos admin interface, Grappelli gives you a consistent and grid-based look & feel and adds some nifty features.

Define Search Options, Filter and Date-Hierarchy based on your contents and your editors needs

Add (custom) Actions and List-Editables for quickly editing contents on the changelist view

Grappelli adds Autocompletes for Foreign Keys, Many-to-Many Relationships and Generic Relations

Grappelli also comes with some jQueryUI–Widgets and includes a minimal theme for TinyMCE

Another feature with Grappelli is the option to reorder inlines (stacked and tabular) with drag & drop.

Finally, with grappelli.dashboard (a stripped–down version of django–admin–tools) you´re able to customize the dashboard.
