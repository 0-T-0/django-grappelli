about

The Django admin interface is a very simple but highly customizable app in order to manage your websites data — some might call it a CMS.

 

On top of Djangos admin interface, Grappelli gives you a consistend and grid-based look & feel and adds some nifty features.

features

Related lookups and Autocompletes for Foreign Keys, M2M– and Generic–Relationships

Customizable dashboard

 

Inline sortables with drag & drop

Integrated TinyMCE theme
